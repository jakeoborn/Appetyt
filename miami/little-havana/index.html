🇨🇺 Little Havana, Miami

13 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 13 Restaurants ✨ Cuban, Latin American, Local Faves

The soul of Miami. Cuban coffee windows, domino parks, and authentic Latin American restaurants along Calle Ocho (8th Street).

Known for: Calle Ocho, Domino Park, ventanitas, cigar shops

Don't miss: Versailles, Ball & Chain, Azucar Ice Cream, El Exquisito

💡 Versailles is the most famous Cuban restaurant in America -- get the vaca frita and a cafecito at the ventanita window. Ball & Chain has live salsa every night.
