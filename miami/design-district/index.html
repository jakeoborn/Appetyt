💎 Design District, Miami

11 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 11 Restaurants ✨ Fine Dining, Luxury Shopping, Special Occasions

Luxury shopping meets high-end dining in a walkable outdoor district. Every major fashion house and some of Miami's best restaurants.

Known for: Louis Vuitton, Cartier, Institute of Contemporary Art

Don't miss: Michael's Genuine, MC Kitchen, Mandolin Aegean Bistro, OTL

💡 Mandolin is set in a 1940s house with a Mediterranean garden -- the best patio in Miami. Book for lunch on a weekday.
