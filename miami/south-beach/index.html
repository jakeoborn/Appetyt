🍽️ 47 Restaurants ✨ Nightlife, Beach Dining, People Watching

Ocean Drive and Collins Avenue packed with hotels, clubs, and restaurants. The iconic Miami experience -- art deco, poolside dining, and late-night energy.

Known for: Ocean Drive, Art Deco Historic District, rooftop pools

Don't miss: Joe's Stone Crab, Juvia, The Surf Club, Prime 112

💡 Joe's Stone Crab does not take reservations -- arrive by 4:30 PM or expect a 2-hour wait. The creamed spinach is almost as famous as the claws.
