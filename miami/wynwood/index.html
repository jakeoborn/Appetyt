🍽️ 37 Restaurants ✨ Street Art, Cocktails, Trendy

Street art capital of the world turned dining and nightlife destination. Warehouse restaurants, craft cocktails, and Art Basel energy year-round.

Known for: Wynwood Walls, murals, galleries, Art Basel

Don't miss: KYU, Alter, Zak the Baker, Coyo Taco

💡 KYU's wood-fired short rib is one of the most famous dishes in Miami. Visit Wynwood Walls during the day and the restaurants at night.
