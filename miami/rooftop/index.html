La Mar by Gastón Acurio
Peruvian / Nikkei · Brickell Key · Forbes Travel Guide, Wine Spectator Award of Excellence
Gastón Acurio's Brickell Key flagship at the Mandarin Oriental — bayside terrace overlooking the Brickell skyline, ceviches and tiraditos that turned Miami onto serious Peruvian cooking.
91
2
Sugar at East Miami
Rooftop Bar / Asian · Brickell
East Miami's 40th-floor rooftop garden — bonsai-bordered cocktail bar with steamed-bao snacks and a 360-degree skyline view of Brickell. The most photographed Brickell rooftop, justifiably.
90
3
Smith & Wollensky Miami Beach
Steakhouse · South Pointe · Wine Spectator Award of Excellence
The white-clapboard chophouse on the southern tip of South Pointe Park — a port-side classic where USDA Prime cuts come with cargo-ship views. The southern bookend to Joe's Stone Crab.
89

Klaw Miami
Seafood / Steakhouse · Edgewater
A multi-level seafood and steak destination inside the former Miami Women's Club — one of the Biscayne waterfront's more theatrical restaurants. A rooftop bar with bay views crowns the building.
88

Rosa Sky Rooftop

AC Hotel Brickell's 12th-floor rooftop — Latin-leaning cocktails, ceviche, and the most photogenic view of Brickell's skyline from below. Built for golden hour.
88
