🍽️ 39 Restaurants ✨ Korean BBQ, Late Night, Karaoke

24-hour Korean BBQ joints, late-night karaoke, and some of the best Asian food in America. The most densely packed dining neighborhood in LA.

Known for: Korean BBQ, all-night dining, karaoke bars, soju

Don't miss: Park's BBQ, Sun Nong Dan, Quarters Korean BBQ, Dan Sung Sa

💡 Park's BBQ is the gold standard -- USDA Prime galbi. Go after 9PM for no wait. Dan Sung Sa is a hidden speakeasy behind an unmarked door.
