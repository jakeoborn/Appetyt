🥢 Chinatown, Las Vegas

60 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 60 Restaurants ✨ Chinese, Japanese, Thai, Korean, Late Night

Spring Mountain Road is the real dining capital of Las Vegas. A dense, mile-long corridor of Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Thai, and Vietnamese restaurants that runs circles around anything on the Strip.

Known for: Spring Mountain Rd, late-night dining, Lotus of Siam, dim sum

Don't miss: Lotus of Siam, Chengdu Taste, District One, Raku

💡 Chinatown is open late -- most spots serve until 2-3 AM. This is where chefs and dealers eat after their shifts.
