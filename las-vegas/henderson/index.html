🌵 Henderson, Las Vegas

26 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 26 Restaurants ✨ Steakhouse, Family, Suburban

Las Vegas's second city, south of the Strip. Green Valley Ranch is the dining anchor. More residential, less tourist, with a growing food scene.

Known for: Green Valley Ranch, Water Street District, Lake Las Vegas

Don't miss: Hank's Fine Steaks, Todd's Unique Dining, Bottiglia

💡 Water Street District in downtown Henderson is the emerging food corridor -- a handful of good spots in a walkable area.
