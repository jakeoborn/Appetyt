24 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 24 Restaurants ✨ Cocktails, Local Scene, Craft Beer

The real Las Vegas. Main Street between Charleston and Sahara is where locals drink, eat, and First Friday. Craft cocktails, breweries, and restaurants without the markup.

Known for: Velveteen Rabbit, Able Baker, First Friday art walk, Esther's Kitchen

Don't miss: Esther's Kitchen, Herbs & Rye, Velveteen Rabbit, Able Baker

💡 First Friday is the monthly art walk -- every gallery opens, food trucks line the street, and the bars stay packed. Go early.
