Top 5 Vietnamese Spots

1
The Black Sheep
Filipino / Asian Fusion · Spring Valley · James Beard Nominee
James Beard nominee chef Jamie Tran's modern Filipino-Vietnamese kitchen. Creative Southeast Asian small plates — umami bomb dishes, serious cocktails, and a quietly confident neighborhood dining room...
91
2
Pho Kim Long
Vietnamese · Chinatown
Chinatown Vietnamese institution on Spring Mountain Rd. Enormous portions of banh mi, pho, com tam, and bun bo hue at rock-bottom prices. One of the most beloved off-Strip Vietnamese spots in Vegas.
88
3
Pho Saigonese
Vietnamese · Chinatown
Chinatown Vietnamese specialist known for its crystal-clear beef pho broth simmered 24+ hours. Family-run, fast-casual, and a local favorite. Second location in south valley.
87
4
District One
Vietnamese · Chinatown
Modern Vietnamese restaurant in Chinatown known for lobster pho and creative takes on Saigon classics. Casual but elevated. One of Vegas's most-loved Vietnamese spots.
87
5
Mekong
Vietnamese / Chinese · Chinatown
Long-running stalwart with clay pot catfish, crispy sizzling beef, and a sprawling lunch locals swear by.
83
