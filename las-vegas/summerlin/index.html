🏜️ Summerlin, Las Vegas

21 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 21 Restaurants ✨ Steakhouse, Family, Scenic

Las Vegas's upscale suburban enclave on the west side. Red Rock Canyon views, master-planned communities, and surprisingly excellent dining anchored by Red Rock Resort.

Known for: Red Rock Canyon, Downtown Summerlin, Red Rock Resort

Don't miss: Echo & Rig, Hearthstone Kitchen, Honey Salt, T-Bones Chophouse

💡 Red Rock Resort casino has some of the best non-Strip dining. Downtown Summerlin is the outdoor mall with restaurants.
