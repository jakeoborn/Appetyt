🎰 Downtown, Las Vegas

30 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 30 Restaurants ✨ Cocktails, History, Casual Dining

Old Vegas reborn. Fremont East is the cocktail and dining corridor, Circa is the new energy, and the Container Park adds family-friendly charm. Grittier and more authentic than the Strip.

Known for: Fremont Street, Circa Stadium Swim, Container Park, neon signs

Don't miss: Carson Kitchen, Le Thai, Esther's Kitchen, Triple George Grill

💡 Fremont East (east of Las Vegas Blvd) is the good part. The covered Fremont Street Experience is touristy but worth one walk-through.
