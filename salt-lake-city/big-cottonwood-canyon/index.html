⛷️ Big Cottonwood Canyon, Salt Lake City

10 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 10 Restaurants ✨ Mountain, Après Ski, Family, Unique

The neighboring canyon to Little Cottonwood, home to Brighton and Solitude. Family-friendly skiing, canyon cabins, and a yurt you snowshoe to for dinner.

Known for: Brighton Resort, Solitude Mountain Resort, Silver Fork Lodge, Lake Mary trail

Don't miss: Silver Fork Lodge, Honeycomb Grill, The Yurt at Solitude

💡 Brighton has the cheapest lift tickets near SLC and night skiing on weekends; Solitude is the quieter sister resort.
