11 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 11 Restaurants ✨ Brewery, Cocktails, New American

A former warehouse district just south of downtown turned brewery and dining hub. Industrial buildings, breweries, and one of the city's top new American spots.

Known for: Industrial warehouses, Fisher Brewing, breweries on 800 South

Don't miss: Urban Hill, Water Witch, Fisher Brewing, R&R BBQ
