🛣️ Kimball Junction, Salt Lake City

19 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 19 Restaurants ✨ Modern American, Italian, Brewpub, Family

The Park City gateway at I-80 exit 145 — outlet shopping, hotels, and a quietly ambitious restaurant row that locals prefer over Main Street crowds.

Known for: Redstone Town Center, Newpark, Swaner Preserve, Utah Olympic Park

Don't miss: Hearth & Hill, Bartolo's, Sterling Steakhouse

💡 Stay at Kimball Junction for cheaper Park City lodging — 10 min to the lifts and walkable dining off the I-80 exit.
