☕ 9th & 9th, Salt Lake City

17 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 17 Restaurants ✨ Mediterranean, Brunch, Coffee

A small bohemian commercial node at the intersection of 900 East and 900 South. Independent shops and a few standout restaurants.

Known for: Tower Theatre, indie boutiques, the 9th & 9th sculpture

Don't miss: Pago, Cafe Niche, La Barba Coffee, Mazza

💡 Pago focuses on hyper-local Utah ingredients and the brunch is one of the best in the city.
