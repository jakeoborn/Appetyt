⛷️ Little Cottonwood Canyon, Salt Lake City

13 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 13 Restaurants ✨ Mountain, Après Ski, Steakhouse

The deep-snow canyon southeast of SLC, home to Alta and Snowbird. Some of the deepest snow on earth and a dozen ski-lodge dining rooms.

Known for: Alta, Snowbird, Cliff Lodge, 550+ inches of annual snowfall

Don't miss: SeventyOne, The Aerie, The Steak Pit

💡 Alta is one of three U.S. ski areas that doesn't allow snowboarders — head to Snowbird next door.
