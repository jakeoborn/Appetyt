Top 10 Ramen & Noodles Spots

1
Mensho
Japanese Ramen · Post District
High-end ramen chain from Tokyo that opened in SLC Post District in 2025. A5 wagyu, fresh daily noodles, and local produce. Some of the best ramen in Utah with bowls up to $45. Worth every penny.
89
2
Koyote
Japanese / Ramen · Downtown SLC · Salt Lake Magazine Best Ramen 2025
The best ramen in Salt Lake City with rich, deeply flavored broths simmered for hours. The brisket is excellent (unusual for a ramen shop), and the chicken wings are a sleeper hit. Casual counter-serv...
87
3
La Cai Noodle House
Vietnamese / Noodles · Westside SLC
Top 10 Best Asian in SLC. The House Noodle Soup has broth described as fishy umami heaven. Authentic Vietnamese noodle dishes at affordable prices.
87
4
Yoko Ramen
Japanese Ramen · Downtown SLC
Downtown ramen shop with rich tonkotsu, spicy miso, and vegetable broths. The gyoza and karaage round out the menu. A cozy counter-service spot for a quick, warming bowl.
86
5
Tosh's Ramen
Japanese Ramen · Holladay
Holladay ramen shop with 18-hour pork bone broth and handmade noodles. The spicy ramen is fiery and the black garlic tonkotsu is rich. One of the original serious ramen shops in the SLC metro.
86
6
One More Noodle House
Chinese Noodles · Downtown SLC
Hand-pulled noodle shop in downtown SLC. Fresh noodles made to order with authentic Chinese flavors. The beef noodle soup is hearty and satisfying.
86
7
Ramen Bar SLC
Japanese Ramen · Downtown SLC
Downtown ramen spot with rich broths and quality noodles.
85
8
Oishi Ramen
Japanese / Ramen · South Salt Lake
South Salt Lake Chinatown ramen bar known for hand-pulled noodles and a diverse menu. Second location now open in the University area (former Sasa Kitchen).
85
9
Hana Ramen Bar
Japanese · Kimball Junction
Small KJ ramen bar with handmade spicy gyoza and broths cooked down 3–5 days. The Kimball cuisine locals rank highest per plate.
83
10
Snow Blossom Bistro
Asian · Big Cottonwood Canyon
Brighton's alpine ramen hideaway off the Crest lift — walk-in or ski-in for steaming bowls at 10,000 ft. The canyon's most surprising on-mountain stop.
80
