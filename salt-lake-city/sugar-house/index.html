🌳 Sugar House, Salt Lake City

52 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 52 Restaurants ✨ Brunch, Casual, Patios

A walkable neighborhood about 15 minutes south of downtown. Sugar House Park, indie shops, and a strong neighborhood dining scene.

Known for: Sugar House Park, 1100 East commercial strip, Sugar House Monument

Don't miss: Hub & Spoke Diner, Matteo Ristorante, Hearth and Hill, Yuki Arashi

💡 Hub & Spoke Diner has the best chicken and waffles in SLC and a no-pretense vibe.
