🦐 Ballard, Seattle

41 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 41 Restaurants ✨ Seafood, Breweries, Brunch

Former Scandinavian fishing village turned brewpub and seafood mecca. Old Ballard Avenue is the dining strip.

Known for: Ballard Ave restaurants, breweries, Sunday farmers market, locks

Don't miss: The Walrus and the Carpenter, Cafe Besalu, Stoneburner, Reuben's Brews

💡 The Ballard Farmers Market (Sundays) is the best in Seattle. Walk from there to Ballard Ave for lunch.
