Self-proclaimed "Center of the Universe." Quirky, creative, with great casual restaurants and the famous Fremont Troll.

Known for: Fremont Troll, Lenin statue, Sunday market, Theo Chocolate factory

Don't miss: Paseo, Revel, Eve, Manolin
