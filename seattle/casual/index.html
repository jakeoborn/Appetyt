Top 10 Casual Dining Spots

1
Musang
Filipino · Beacon Hill · James Beard Semifinalist 2026
Chef Melissa Miranda's Beacon Hill Filipino restaurant. Community-driven dining with kamayan feasts and a la carte dishes celebrating Filipino heritage with PNW ingredients.
93
2
Rupee Bar
Indian Street Food · Ballard · James Beard Semifinalist
Chef Sapna Punjabi-Gupta's vibrant Indian street food bar on Capitol Hill. Chaat, dosas, and creative cocktails in a fun, casual setting. One of Seattle's most exciting restaurants.
91
3
Din Tai Fung
Taiwanese / Dumplings · University Village
The Seattle outpost of the legendary Taiwanese dumpling house. Known worldwide for perfect xiao long bao (soup dumplings). Lines form daily but move fast.
91
4
Paseo
Caribbean / Cuban · Fremont
Seattle's legendary Caribbean sandwich shop. The roasted pork shoulder pressed sandwich on a toasted baguette is frequently called the best sandwich in Seattle. Cash only.
91
5
Un Bien
Caribbean · Ballard
Caribbean sandwich shop in Ballard famous for the Caribbean roast sandwich -- slow-cooked pork with caramelized onions. A cult Seattle favorite known for long lines and generous portions.
91
6
Local Tide
Seafood · Fremont
Fremont seafood spot known for innovative Pacific Northwest dishes. Highlights range from pork fat-laced shrimp toast to a mayo-y Dungeness crab roll. Casual counter service with creative daily specia...
91
7
Taylor Shellfish Farms
Seafood / Oyster Bar · Capitol Hill
Family-owned since 1890 — the oldest shellfish farm in Washington state. Capitol Hill outpost serving pristine oysters, Dungeness crab, and geoduck straight from their farms.
90
8
Kedai Makan
Malaysian · Capitol Hill · Eater Seattle Essential
Authentic Malaysian street food on Capitol Hill. Nasi lemak, laksa, and roti canai made with precision and care. One of the most unique restaurants in the Pacific Northwest.
90
9
Cafe Besalu
French Bakery · Ballard
Beloved Ballard French bakery known for the best croissants in Seattle. Everything is made by hand daily — when they sell out, they close. Lines form before opening.
90
10
Cafe Suliman
East African · Capitol Hill · James Beard Emerging Chef Semifinalist 2026
Ahmed Suliman's casual Capitol Hill spot in Melrose Market. East African-inspired dishes that earned a 2026 James Beard Emerging Chef semifinalist nod. One of Seattle's most exciting new voices.
90
