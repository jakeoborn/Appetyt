Top 8 BBQ Spots

1
Jack's BBQ
Texas BBQ · Georgetown
Traditional Central Texas BBQ in SoDo/Georgetown since 2014. Low-and-slow dry rub brisket, ribs, and smoked meats with sauce on the side. Jack's dream to bring authentic Texas BBQ to Seattle.
88
2
Kau Kau Barbeque
Chinese / BBQ · Chinatown-International District
Seattle's legendary Chinese BBQ since 1974 — lacquered ducks hanging in the window, BBQ pork, and shiny-skin roast duck to go or eat in. A CID rite of passage.
88
3
K-BBQ Woobling
Korean BBQ · Bellevue
Upscale Bellevue Korean BBQ with theatrical presentation — premium proteins arrive curled like roses on wooden boards, grilled tableside in a sleek, modern setting.
88
4
MEET Korean BBQ
Korean BBQ · Capitol Hill
Tableside Korean BBQ grilling wagyu and kurobuta pork in Capitol Hill. Quality meats and traditional banchan spread. Resy Hit List Winter 2026.
87
5
Lady Jaye
American / Smokehouse · West Seattle
West Seattle Junction smokehouse and cocktail bar. Wagyu smash burgers, ribs, whiskey focus, and a lively neighborhood corner on California Ave. Owned by the team behind Rocksport.
87
6
Outsider BBQ
BBQ / American · Fremont
Fremont outdoor BBQ joint with a beer garden — slow-smoked brisket, pulled pork, and ribs in a laid-back setting that draws lines on sunny Seattle weekends.
85
7
Wood Shop BBQ
BBQ · Columbia City
Columbia City BBQ joint with slow-smoked brisket, ribs, and pulled pork. House-made rubs, solid sides, and a neighborhood feel.
85
8
Pecos Pit BBQ
BBQ · SODO
SODO BBQ sandwich window since 1980. Cash only, lines long, and a sandwich that's pure smoky beef comfort. A Seattle institution.
83
