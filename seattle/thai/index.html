Wild Ginger

Pan-Asian destination since 1989 — satay bar, Burmese, Thai, and Chinese regional dishes. Downtown flagship plus a Bellevue second. A Seattle benchmark for Asian fine dining with longevity.

Thai Tom
Thai · University District
Legendary wok-fired Thai counter on the Ave — open kitchen, blazing heat, and intensely flavorful curries and stir-fries that have defined the U-District for decades.

5
Aroy Mak Thai Food
Thai · Greenwood
Greenwood Thai family restaurant built on 40-year family recipes. Named to Yelp's Top 100 US Restaurants 2026 list. Small focused menu, huge flavors, beloved by locals.
88

Sisi Kay Thai Eatery & Bar
Thai · Wallingford
Two-story Wallingford Thai restaurant (formerly May Restaurant) with punchy noodles and thick, vibrant curries. Roasted duck curry is an instant mood-booster. Run by the same family for over a decade.
87
7
Pestle Rock Isan Thai
Thai · Ballard
Ballard Isan-Thai specialist with northeastern Thai dishes you rarely see in Seattle. Som tum, larb, grilled meats. Authentic and spicy.

Thai · Downtown
Downtown Seattle upscale Thai in a sleek dining room. Royal Thai plates, creative cocktails, and Westlake area location.

Ayutthaya Thai
Thai · Capitol Hill
Capitol Hill Thai staple since the late '80s. Classic dishes — pad kee mao, green papaya salad, green curry. Unpretentious, consistent, beloved by the neighborhood.
