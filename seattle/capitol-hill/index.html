🎸 Capitol Hill, Seattle

102 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 102 Restaurants ✨ Nightlife, Cocktails, Diverse Dining

Seattle's cultural hub. Bars, restaurants, live music, and the city's LGBTQ+ heart. The densest dining neighborhood in the Pacific Northwest.

Known for: Pike/Pine corridor, independent bookshops, live music, cocktail bars

Don't miss: Cascina Spinasse, Rupee Bar, Le Caviste, Taylor Shellfish, Canon (whiskey bar)

💡 Walk Pike/Pine between Broadway and 12th Ave for the best concentration of restaurants and bars.
