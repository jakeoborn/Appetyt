🏡 Wallingford, Seattle

16 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 16 Restaurants ✨ Date Night, Neighborhood Gems

A residential neighborhood with surprisingly excellent restaurants along 45th Street. Where Seattle chefs eat on their nights off.

Known for: Joule, Westward waterfront, Stone Way restaurants

Don't miss: Joule, Westward, Kisaku Sushi, Brimmer & Heeltap

💡 Westward's heated waterfront patio with fire pits is magical — book for sunset.
