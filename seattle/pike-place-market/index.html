🐟 Pike Place Market, Seattle

23 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 23 Restaurants ✨ Local Favorites, Quick Bite, Seafood

The soul of Seattle since 1907. Fish throwing, flower stands, and the original Starbucks. Tourist-heavy but still essential.

Known for: Fish Market, flower stands, Pike Place Chowder, original Starbucks

Don't miss: Pike Place Chowder, Piroshky Piroshky, Matt's in the Market, Beecher's Cheese

💡 Go before 9 AM to see the market like locals do. The lower levels (DownUnder) have hidden gems.
