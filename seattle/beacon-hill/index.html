🌏 Beacon Hill, Seattle

14 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 14 Restaurants ✨ International, Fine Dining, Local Favorites

Seattle's most diverse neighborhood. Filipino, Vietnamese, Ethiopian, and cutting-edge fine dining side by side along Beacon Ave.

Known for: Archipelago, Musang, Homer, Light Rail access, diverse food scene

Don't miss: Archipelago, Musang, Homer, Perihelion Brewery

💡 Archipelago only seats 8 per night — book months ahead. Musang's kamayan feast is a communal dining experience.
