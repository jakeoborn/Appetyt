🍽️ 160 Restaurants ✨ BBQ, Tacos, Date Night, Cocktails

Austin's creative dining capital. Once industrial, now home to Franklin Barbecue and the city's most acclaimed taquerias and chef-driven concepts.

Known for: Franklin Barbecue, East 6th Street, murals, food trucks

Don't miss: Franklin Barbecue, Suerte, Nixta Taqueria, Birdie's

💡 Franklin opens at 11am and the line forms before sunrise — Birdie's is walk-in for natural wine and pasta.
