Known for: Dell Diamond, Round Rock Premium Outlets, Dell HQ

Don't miss: Uchi Round Rock, Salt Traders Coastal Cooking

Round Rock outpost of Jack Gilmore's Texas-flavored Southern farm-to-table. Wood-fire-finished entrees, jalapeño cornbread, and a regular brunch crowd that books out the patio.

Seafood
Sister concept to Jack Allen's Kitchen — Gulf-coast seafood with raw bar, mesquite-grilled fish, and Hill Country-meets-coastal-Texas plating in a laid-back room off IH-35.
