10 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 10 Restaurants ✨ Brunch, Italian, Bakery

One of Austin's oldest neighborhoods, just north of UT. Bungalows, Quack's bakery, and neighborhood-focused dining.

Known for: Avenue B, Shipe Park, historic bungalows

Don't miss: Lutie's, Asti Trattoria, Quack's 43rd Street Bakery

💡 Lutie's is inside the Commodore Perry Estate — the garden tasting menu is one of the best in Austin.
