🌵 South Congress, Austin

39 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 39 Restaurants ✨ Brunch, Patios, Shopping

SoCo. The walkable strip of boutiques, vintage shops, and patio restaurants south of the river with the iconic Capitol view.

Known for: South Congress Avenue, "I love you so much" mural, Hotel San Jose

Don't miss: Terry Black's Barbecue, Home Slice Pizza, Perla's, Aba

💡 Walk SoCo from the river south — the Capitol view from Annie Street is the best photo on the strip.
