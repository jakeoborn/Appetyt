Top 10 Rooftop & Views Spots

1
Fahrenheit
Steakhouse / Rooftop · Uptown
Chef Rocco Whalen's 21st-floor rooftop steakhouse atop the Skye Condos — sweeping skyline views, dry-aged steaks, and a sushi program that punches above its address.
88
2
Cloud Bar by David Burke
Rooftop Bar / Modern American · Uptown
Chef David Burke's rooftop bar atop the JW Marriott Charlotte — high-floor skyline views, theatrical small plates, and a cocktail program built around Burke's signature whimsy.
86
3
Merchant & Trade
Rooftop Bar · Uptown
The rooftop bar atop the Kimpton Tryon Park — 19 stories of skyline, a strong cocktail program, and one of Charlotte's most consistent rooftop scenes.
86
4
Nuvole Rooftop TwentyTwo
Italian / Rooftop · Uptown
A 22nd-floor Italian rooftop concept above the Vue tower — wood-fired pizza, handmade pasta, and a long bar pulled up to the floor-to-ceiling skyline.
86
5
Hestia Rooftop
Mediterranean / Rooftop · Ballantyne
A 16th-floor Mediterranean rooftop restaurant in Ballantyne Village — panoramic south-Charlotte views, a wood-fired menu, and the best rooftop dining south of Uptown.
85
6
Buho
Mexican / Rooftop · Uptown
An Uptown rooftop Mexican concept with mezcal-forward cocktails and a wood-fired menu — a buzzy after-work scene and one of the city's highest-volume agave programs.
85
7
Mariposa
Modern American · Uptown
Modern American dining on the top of the Nordstrom at SouthPark's sister location in Uptown — Mariposa has been a Nordstrom in-store dining tradition for decades, now a destination.
84
8
Aura Rooftop
Rooftop Bar / American · Uptown
An Uptown rooftop with skyline views and a wraparound patio — popular for after-work drinks and weekend scene nights.
84
9
Novelty House
Rooftop Bar / Cocktails · Uptown
An Uptown rooftop and cocktail concept on the upper floors of one of Tryon's modern towers — designed for the long after-work hour with a tight, vermouth-leaning bar program.
84
10
North Harbor Club
American / Waterfront · Davidson
A Davidson-side Lake Norman waterfront restaurant with a long dock for boat-up dining — seafood, steaks, and one of the metro's most reliable sunset patios.
84
