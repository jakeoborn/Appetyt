🛍️ Rice Village, Houston

14 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 14 Restaurants ✨ Patios, Cocktails, Brunch

A walkable retail and dining district adjacent to Rice University. Boutiques, patios, and a strong tiki/cocktail scene.

Known for: Rice University, Rice Village shops, walkable blocks

Don't miss: Helen Greek Food and Wine, The Toasted Coconut, Milton's

💡 Rice Village is one of the only walkable retail districts inside the Loop — park once and explore.
