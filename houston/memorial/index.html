🌲 Memorial, Houston

22 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 22 Restaurants ✨ Steakhouse, Date Night, Family

A wooded west-side residential area along Memorial Drive. Memorial Park, the Houston Arboretum, and a cluster of upscale Texan and steakhouse spots.

Known for: Memorial Park, Houston Arboretum, Memorial Drive, residential canopy

Don't miss: Credence, Rainbow Lodge, Taste of Texas

💡 Rainbow Lodge is in a 1900s log cabin tucked into the woods — easy to miss but worth finding.
