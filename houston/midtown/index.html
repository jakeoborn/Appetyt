🍤 Midtown, Houston

31 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 31 Restaurants ✨ Brunch, Cocktails, Nightlife

The neighborhood between Downtown and the Museum District. A dense mix of nightlife, brunch, and Houston's most famous breakfast spot.

Known for: Bagby Park, midtown bars, Vietnamese cafes

Don't miss: The Breakfast Klub, Brennan's of Houston, Indigo, Late August

💡 The Breakfast Klub line wraps the building on weekends — go on a Tuesday morning.
