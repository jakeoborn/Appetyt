Top 9 Happy Hour Spots

1
The Esquire Tavern
American Gastropub · River Walk
The longest wooden bar top in Texas, right on the River Walk. Since 1933, this historic tavern serves craft cocktails and elevated pub fare. A San Antonio icon with serious drinks.
92
2
La Gloria
Mexican Street Food · Pearl District
Chef Johnny Hernandez's beloved tribute to Mexican street food at the Pearl. Authentic regional dishes from across Mexico in a lively, colorful setting with great margaritas.
91
3
Southerleigh Fine Food & Brewery
Southern American · Pearl District
Housed in the historic Pearl Brewery building, Southerleigh pairs craft beers brewed on-site with refined Southern cuisine. Beautiful industrial-chic space with a buzzy atmosphere.
90
4
Sangria on the Burg
Mexican & Cocktails · Alamo Heights
A lively Alamo Heights hangout known for creative sangrias, solid Mexican fare, and one of the best patios in the neighborhood. Popular weekend brunch destination.
89
5
Rosario's Mexican Cafe y Cantina
Mexican · Southtown
Lisa Wong's Southtown institution since 1992. Tex-Mex with interior Mexican flair, famous for puffy tacos, enchiladas, and one of the best patios in San Antonio.
89
6
Sternewirth at Hotel Emma
American Gastropub · Pearl District
The atmospheric bar and lounge inside Hotel Emma at the Pearl. Craft cocktails served in the gorgeously restored 19th-century brewhouse with original industrial details intact.
89
7
2Bros BBQ Market
Texas BBQ · North San Antonio
Jason and Jake Dady's beloved BBQ joint in a converted gas station. Affordable, no-frills smoked meats with creative daily specials. A neighborhood favorite with loyal regulars.
88
8
The Luxury
American Bar & Grill · St. Mary's Strip
The anchor of the St. Mary's Strip nightlife scene. Craft cocktails, solid bar food, and a great outdoor area. A favorite gathering spot for San Antonio's creative crowd.
88
9
Shuck Shack
Seafood / Oyster Bar · Pearl District
Casual oyster bar and seafood shack at the Pearl. Fresh Gulf oysters, peel-and-eat shrimp, and cold beer on a shaded patio. Perfect San Antonio happy hour spot.
88
