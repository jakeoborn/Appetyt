🎷 Uptown, Chicago

18 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 18 Restaurants ✨ Jazz, Vietnamese, International

A historically diverse neighborhood on the north side. Jazz history, Vietnamese restaurants, and a renaissance dining scene.

Known for: Green Mill, Uptown Theatre, Argyle Street Vietnamese strip

Don't miss: Green Mill Cocktail Lounge, Cariño, Nha Hang Viet Nam

💡 The Green Mill was one of Al Capone's favorite haunts and still hosts jazz nightly.
