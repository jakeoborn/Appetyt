🏮 Chinatown, Chicago

13 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 13 Restaurants ✨ Chinese, Dim Sum, Cheap Eats

One of the oldest and largest Chinatowns in the US with authentic Cantonese, Sichuan, and dim sum that rivals either coast.

Known for: Chinatown Square, dim sum halls, bakeries

Don't miss: MingHin Cuisine, Lao Sze Chuan, Chiu Quon Bakery

💡 MingHin for dim sum on weekends -- go before 11 AM to avoid the massive line. The bakeries on Wentworth have $1 pork buns.
