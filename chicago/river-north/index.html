🏙️ River North, Chicago

64 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 64 Restaurants ✨ Steakhouses, Deep Dish, Nightlife

Chicago flashiest dining neighborhood with steakhouses, deep dish, and nightlife. Tourist-heavy but some genuine gems.

Known for: Lou Malnati's, Bavette's, Portillo's, RPM Italian

Don't miss: Bavette's, Joe's, Frontera Grill, Portillo's

💡 Avoid the generic sports bars on Division. The real restaurants are on Kinzie and Hubbard.
