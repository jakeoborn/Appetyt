🔥 West Loop / Fulton Market, Chicago

55 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 55 Restaurants ✨ Fine Dining, Trendy, Date Night

The undisputed dining capital of Chicago. Randolph Street and Fulton Market pack more Michelin stars per block than anywhere in the Midwest.

Known for: Girl & the Goat, Avec, Au Cheval, Publican

Don't miss: Monteverde, Smyth, Girl & the Goat, Au Cheval

💡 Restaurant Row is Randolph between Halsted and Ogden. Book ahead -- walk-ins are tough.
