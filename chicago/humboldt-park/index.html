6 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 6 Restaurants ✨ Omakase, French, Vegan

A historically Puerto Rican neighborhood on the west side. Now home to a hidden omakase counter and a beloved French cafe.

Known for: Humboldt Park itself, Puerto Rican community, Paseo Boricua

Don't miss: Kai Zan, Cafe Marie-Jeanne, Where Roots Reside
