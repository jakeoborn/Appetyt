The neighborhood between Wicker Park and the West Loop. Mostly residential with a strong concentration of chef-driven destinations.

Known for: Chicago Avenue, residential blocks, art galleries

Don't miss: Feld, Coalfire Pizza, Arami, Boeufhaus
