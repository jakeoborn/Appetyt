12 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 12 Restaurants ✨ Filipino, Bakery, Quiet

A historic Ukrainian immigrant neighborhood west of Wicker Park. Quiet residential streets with destination Filipino tasting menu and gelato.

Known for: Ukrainian Cultural Center, residential blocks, Chicago Avenue

Don't miss: Kasama, Black Dog Gelato
