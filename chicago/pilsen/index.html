🇲🇽 Pilsen, Chicago

15 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 15 Restaurants ✨ Mexican, Street Food, Art, Local Favorites

Chicago most vibrant Mexican neighborhood with incredible street art, authentic taquerias, and a growing creative scene.

Known for: 18th Street murals, National Museum of Mexican Art, taquerias

Don't miss: Don Pedro Carnitas, Dusek's, Pilsen Rooftop

💡 Walk 18th Street for the murals and taquerias. The National Museum of Mexican Art is free and outstanding.
