🚗 Forney, Dallas

5 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 5 Restaurants ✨ BBQ, Family, Casual

A small Kaufman County town just east of Dallas. A handful of family-run restaurants and Texas-style BBQ joints.

Known for: Small-town Texas feel, FM 548, Forney High School

Don't miss: Soulman's Bar-B-Que, Mama's Daughters' Diner, Conti Seafood Eatery

💡 Soulman's started in Greenville and Forney is one of the original locations.
