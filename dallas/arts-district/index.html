🏛️ Arts District, Dallas

9 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 9 Restaurants ✨ Fine Dining, Pre-Concert, Special Occasions

The cultural core of Dallas. Museums, concert halls, and some very serious restaurants. Quieter on weekends -- this is where Dallas goes to be refined.

Known for: AT&T Performing Arts Center, Nasher Sculpture Center, Tei-An

Don't miss: Tei-An, Bullion, Stephan Pyles Flora Street Cafe, Margaux

💡 Book Tei-An before a Winspear Opera House show. The soba and sake combination is one of Dallas best dinners.
