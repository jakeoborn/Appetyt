🎨 Bishop Arts, Dallas

28 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 28 Restaurants ✨ Date Night, Local Faves, Brunch

Oak Cliff independent arts and dining district. 60+ locally owned shops and restaurants in a walkable 6-block area.

Known for: James Beard nominees, chef-driven concepts, walkable streets

Don't miss: Lucia, Enos Pizza Tavern, Oddfellows, Lockhart Smokehouse

💡 Go on a weekday evening -- weekends get extremely crowded and parking is tough.
