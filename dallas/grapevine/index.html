🍷 Grapevine, Dallas

10 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 10 Restaurants ✨ Wine, Family, Historic

A historic Main Street town and Texas wine country gateway. Wineries, the Gaylord Texan, and a walkable downtown anchor it.

Known for: Historic Main Street, Grapevine wineries, Gaylord Texan, Grapevine Mills

Don't miss: Main Street Bistro & Bakery, Esparza's, Dino's Steak and Claw House

💡 Most Grapevine wineries have downtown tasting rooms within walking distance of each other.
