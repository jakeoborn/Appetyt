🏗️ Cedars, Dallas

10 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 10 Restaurants ✨ Craft Beer, Creative Dining, Art Scene

South of downtown, the Cedars is Dallas emerging creative district. Former warehouses now house breweries, art spaces, and some of the city most exciting restaurants.

Known for: Written by the Seasons, Four Corners Brewing, Full City Rooster, Lee Harvey's

Don't miss: Written by the Seasons, Lee Harvey's, Four Corners Brewing

💡 Lee Harvey's outdoor patio is one of the best dive bar patios in Dallas. Written by the Seasons is a must-book.
