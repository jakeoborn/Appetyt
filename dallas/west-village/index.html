🛍️ West Village, Dallas

10 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 10 Restaurants ✨ Brunch, Shopping + Dining, Casual

Uptown walkable village with a European feel. Tree-lined streets, boutique shopping, and a dense concentration of restaurants and cafes. The McKinney Ave Trolley runs right through.

Known for: Malai Kitchen, The Rustic, Sweetgreen, San Martin Bakery, Violet Crown Cinema

Don't miss: Malai Kitchen, Salum, The Rustic, Ka Thai

💡 Park once and walk -- everything is within a few blocks. The McKinney Ave Trolley is free and connects to Uptown bars south.
