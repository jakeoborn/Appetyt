🍴 Addison, Dallas

24 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 24 Restaurants ✨ Steakhouse, BBQ, Family

A small north Dallas suburb famous for having more restaurants per capita than almost anywhere in Texas. Steakhouses, BBQ, and chains anchor it.

Known for: Addison Circle, Belt Line Road, Kaboom Town fireworks

Don't miss: Chamberlain's Steak and Fish, Cattleack Barbeque, Kenny's Italian Kitchen

💡 Cattleack Barbeque is only open Thursday and Friday lunch — get there before 11am.
