10 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 10 Restaurants ✨ Family, Casual, Wine Bars

A growing northern suburb on Lake Grapevine. Family-friendly dining at chain restaurants and a few standout independents.

Known for: Lake Grapevine, suburban subdivisions, parks

Don't miss: Rustico Wood Fired Grill, Mi Dia From Scratch, Bonchon

💡 Most of the dining is in shopping centers along FM 2499 and Long Prairie Road.
