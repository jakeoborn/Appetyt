🌉 Trinity Groves, Dallas

13 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 13 Restaurants ✨ Patios, Views, Casual

A small West Dallas restaurant park at the foot of the Margaret Hunt Hill Bridge. Patios, skyline views, and a rotating mix of concepts.

Known for: Margaret Hunt Hill Bridge, skyline views, Singleton Boulevard

Don't miss: El Carlos Elegante, Kate Weiser Chocolate, Beto & Son

💡 The bridge view from Trinity Groves at sunset is one of the best in Dallas.
