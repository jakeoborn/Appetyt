🌲 Lake Highlands, Dallas

10 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 10 Restaurants ✨ Neighborhood Dining, Craft Beer, Casual

Northeast Dallas neighborhood with a growing food and drink scene anchored by the Walnut Hill corridor. Local favorites, craft breweries, and the kind of neighborhood spots where regulars are the norm.

Known for: Goldie's, Vector Brewing, Mariano's Hacienda

Don't miss: Goldie's, Vector Brewing

💡 The Walnut Hill Ln corridor near Audelia has the best concentration. Goldie's is worth the drive from anywhere in Dallas.
