Known for: Lake Ray Hubbard, The Harbor district, Rockwall County Courthouse

Don't miss: Zanata, Bin 303, Sear Steakhouse & Lounge, Dodie's Cajun Diner

💡 The Harbor patios face west — go for sunset over the lake.
