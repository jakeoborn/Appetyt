🏙️ Uptown, Dallas

68 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 68 Restaurants ✨ Happy Hour, Brunch, Nightlife

Dallas most walkable neighborhood. Bars, brunch spots, and patios for days. McKinney Avenue is the main artery.

Known for: Katy Trail access, rooftop bars, the young professional crowd

Don't miss: Midnight Rambler, Las Palmas, Uchi Dallas, Komali

💡 Park once and walk everywhere. The M-Line Trolley runs along McKinney Avenue free of charge.
