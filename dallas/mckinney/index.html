9 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 9 Restaurants ✨ Family, Brunch, Local Faves

Historic downtown McKinney has one of the best-preserved Texas town squares, surrounded by independent restaurants and bars.

Known for: Historic Downtown Square, Tupps Brewery, Adriatica Village

Don't miss: Harvest at the Masonic, Patina Green, Local Yocal BBQ

💡 Park once on the square and walk — most of the good spots are within two blocks.
