🌿 Knox-Henderson, Dallas

42 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 42 Restaurants ✨ Cocktails, Date Night, Upscale Casual

Trendy stretch between Highland Park and Uptown. Think boutiques, cocktail bars, and some of Dallas best new restaurants.

Known for: Tei Tei Robata Bar, upscale dining, Knox Street shopping

Don't miss: Tei Tei Robata Bar, Gemma, Highland Park Pharmacy, Bowen House

💡 Knox Street has free parking behind most restaurants. Gemma is one of the best new openings on Henderson.
