🌳 Oak Cliff, Dallas

15 restaurants · 2026

🍽️ 15 Restaurants ✨ Local Faves, Tacos, Craft Beer

South Dallas neighborhoods with deep character. Bishop Arts is the dining hub, but the broader Oak Cliff area has great tacos, dives, and community spots.

Known for: Independent spirit, diverse food scene, Oak Cliff Brewing Co.

Don't miss: Lucia, Lockhart Smokehouse, Oak Cliff Brewing Co.

💡 Oak Cliff Brewing is right by the DART Red Line -- grab a beer on the patio after exploring Bishop Arts.
