🎵 Deep Ellum, Dallas

44 curated restaurants · 2026 Guide

🍽️ 44 Restaurants ✨ Live Music, Late Night, Casual Dining, Craft Beer

Dallas historic arts and music district. Live music 7 nights a week. Murals everywhere. The most electric neighborhood in Dallas after dark.

Known for: Pecan Lodge BBQ, street art murals, blues and jazz venues, Ghost Donkey

Don't miss: Pecan Lodge, Tatsu Dallas, Ruins, Community Beer Co.

💡 The strip is best Fri and Sat after 9PM. Start at Ruins for cocktails, then bar hop down Elm and Main. Ghost Donkey for mezcal.
